1. What Personal Data We Collect
We collect personal data in a lawful, fair manner, limited to what is necessary for the processing purposes, including:

1.1 Data You Provide Directly

Customer Service Data: Information you provide when contacting customer support, submitting feedback, or participating in surveys (e.g., name, email address).

1.2 Data We Collect Automatically
When you experience our games, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect:

Identifier Data: User identifiers, device IDs (Android/iOS ID), advertising IDs (Google Ad ID, IDFA), and IP addresses to determine country/region.

Device & Technical Data: Device model, system language, operating system version, network information, and storage status.

Game Data: Gameplay progress, achievements, session duration, and crash logs.

Ad Interaction Data: History of viewing or clicking on in-game ads to measure effectiveness and prevent fraud.

1.3 Data from Third Parties

Social Media Logins: If you log in using Google or Apple ID, we receive basic authentication data subject to these platforms' policies.

App Stores (Google Play / App Store): When you make In-App Purchases, we receive information about the order ID and transaction status. We do not collect or store your credit card information.

Note: Our Services do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (such as biometric data, health information, or racial origin).

2. How We Use Your Data
We use the collected information for the following primary purposes:

Provide and Maintain Services: Ensure stable game performance, save gameplay progress, and verify in-game transactions.

Improve Experience: Analyze user behavior and detect bugs to optimize game performance.

Ad Distribution & Monetization: Display relevant and personalized advertisements through partner ad networks (such as Google AdMob, IronSource, etc.).

Customer Support: Receive and resolve your inquiries and complaints via email.

Ensure Security: Prevent fraudulent activities (cheating/hacking) and protect our systems.

Legal Compliance: Meet lawful requests from authorities or distribution platforms (Google Play, App Store).

3. How We Share Your Data
We do not sell your personal data. However, to keep our games running smoothly and maintain revenue, we may share data with the following partners:

Technical Service Providers: Cloud storage platforms, Data Analytics services, and Crash Reporting systems.

Advertising Partners (Ad Networks): We integrate third-party SDKs (like AdMob, Mediation platforms) to serve ads. These partners may collect your Advertising ID and IP address to deliver personalized ads. You can opt out of this feature in your device settings or through the consent dialog (e.g., Google UMP, ATT) when launching the game.

Legal Obligations: If required by court order or to protect the legitimate rights of Titan Game Studio.

4. Data Transfer, Security, and Retention
International Transfer: Your data may be stored and processed on servers provided by third parties in various countries. We always comply with applicable data protection regulations when doing so.

Security: We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. However, no internet transmission system is 100% secure.

Retention: We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, or until you request its deletion.

5. Your Legal Rights
Depending on your region (such as Europe with GDPR, or California with CCPA), you may have the following rights regarding your data:

Right to Access & Rectification: Request a copy of your personal data or update inaccurate information.

Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request us to delete all your personal data and game progress from our systems.

To exercise any of the above rights, please send a request to: t.p.tai93@gmail.com. We will respond as soon as possible in accordance with the law.
